The XSS-injection-esque characters at the left border of the source code
copyright statements is actually a little ASCII-art of a shield, if you squint
hard enough.

You need to add some whitespace and also use "a lot" of imagination to see it.

This process is a homage to the project's predecessor, Aegis, archived at:
https://github.com/shieldworks/aegis
